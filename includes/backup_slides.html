  • For any convex form $p: \mathbb R^n \rightarrow \mathbb R$ of degree $2d$: \[\frac1{(2d)!} \nabla^dp(y) \cdot x^{\circ d} \le A_{2d} \sqrt{p(x) p(y)} \quad \forall x, y \in \mathbb R^n\]
  • $A_{2d} = $ solution to an SDP
  • For $d=1$, \(Q \succeq 0 \Rightarrow x^TQy \le \sqrt{x^TQx}\sqrt{y^TQy}\)
    • $A_2, A_4 = 1$
    • $A_6 = 1$
    • $A_8 = 1.01...$ root of $t^3 - \frac{33}{35}t^2 - \frac{17}{245}t + \frac{13}{42875}$
